1) Cut all required vegetables as per proportion of quantity.
2) Set pasta for boiling for 10-11 minutes.
3) Start cooking vegetables with olive oil.
4) Put tomatoes in boiling water for 10-15 seconds after making slashes and bring them out, peel the skin and dice finely.
5) Start cooking tomatoes with onion and garlic for making sauce.
6) One all are cooked, mix, cook for a few minutes together with seasoning to taste.
